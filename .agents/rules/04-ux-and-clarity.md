# Rule: Favor Readable, Trustworthy UX

## UX priorities

- Fast time-to-fun beats ornamental complexity.
- The player should always understand what changed, why it changed, and what they can do next.
- Combat must remain readable under pressure.
- Mobile responsiveness and accessibility are important, but should not destabilize the core loop.

## Anti-goals

Avoid:

- dense panels that bury critical state;
- excessive animation that obscures turn flow;
- decorative immersion features that outpace core mechanics;
- verbose AI text that removes player agency.

## Required communication to the player

Whenever relevant, the UX should make clear:

- current actor / turn;
- action result;
- state delta;
- available next action.
