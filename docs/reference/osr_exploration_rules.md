# OSR / AD&D 1e Exploration Mechanics
- **Time Scale**: 1 Dungeon Turn = 10 minutes. 6 Turns = 1 Hour.
- **Light Sources**: Torch = lasts 6 Turns. Oil Flask (Lantern) = lasts 24 Turns.
- **Random Encounters**: Roll 1d6 every 2 Turns. A result of 1 triggers an encounter. Loud actions force an immediate check.
- **Resting**: The party must rest 1 Turn out of every 6. Skipping rest applies fatigue penalties.
- **Rations**: 1 Ration consumed per character per 24 hours (144 Turns).
